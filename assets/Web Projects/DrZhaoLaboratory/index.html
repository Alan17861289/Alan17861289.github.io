Introduction of Laboratory

Welcome to Dr. Jing Yang’s Lab at the National Center for Protein Sciences (The Phoenix Center, Beijing). Our Lab develops quantitative and site-centric chemoproteomic technology. We use these tools to answer fundamental questions in redox biology and to help discover 'druggable' targets in native proteomes. We are continuously looking for self motivated individuals who share our research interests and my Lab philosophy "Work Hard, Play Harder". Queries on graduate, postdoctoral, and research staff positions can be made to Dr Jing Yang directly.
