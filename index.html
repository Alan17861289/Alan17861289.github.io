Invasion–Sky Has Fallen

Ditagal Design Poster
A2 size

Invasion–Sky Has Fallen

This is a set of community posters. In our society, people keep destroying animals’ habitats for development. In this project, the spheres represent different habitats. For animals, their habitats are like their home planets. Businessmen are like evil invaders in films, invading animals’ planets and destroying their homes. Those businessmen tend to control everything in the nature, they think they are the authoritarian kings. Their behaviors bring land, water and light pollution. Animals are eventually losing their homes and disappearing. I imitate the style of WWF’s posters, in which graphics instead of texts are mainly used as images can attract audience. I hope this project can let people know more about the plights faced by animals thus they will stop destroying their natural habitats.

I went to Victoria Harbour to take a panorama, and then used PhotoShop to create a polar panorama.

My Cells, My-Self

Digital Design
110cm X 100cm

My Cells, My-Self

We are made up of thousands of cells. There are different types of cells which have different functions. Under the microscope, cells are beautiful and have pretty structures. I make use of the images of cells ( like white blood cells, red blood cells and skin cells) to create my portrait. It can show the complexity of human beings and the wonder of the nature. For me, it also shows the grace from God.
